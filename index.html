Longboarding and Kansas City

About longboarding

Longboarding is many things. It is the action of foot to ground, propelling yourself forward, up, down. It is flowing through your environment only limited by your capabilities, and only in part by gear.

Longboarding is also a way to build community, and to foster joy and creativity.

So, what makes Kansas City special?

There's a few things that make Kansas City special

  1. There are many hills to push up AND bomb down.
  2. There are plenty of flats/slight downhills to get some pumping action in on.
  3. The flow of the whole city is magical.
